Top Budget Travel Tips

  1. Travel Off-Season - Avoid peak times for cheaper flights & accommodations
  2. Use Public Transport - Buses and trains are often cheaper than taxis
  3. Stay in Hostels - Save money while meeting fellow travelers
  4. Eat Like a Local - Street food is delicious and budget-friendly
  5. Book in Advance - Early bird discounts on flights and tours
